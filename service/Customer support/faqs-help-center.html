FAQs & Help Center

Find quick answers to commonly asked questions related to bookings, payments, cancellations, and vehicle usage.

How do I book a car?

+

You can book a car by selecting a vehicle, choosing a rental plan, and completing the booking form with required details.

What documents are required for booking?

+

A valid driving license and a government-issued ID proof are required at the time of booking.

What payment methods are accepted?

+

We accept debit cards, credit cards, and online payment methods.

How long does it take to receive a refund?

+

Refunds are usually processed within 3–5 business days, depending on the payment method used.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

+

Yes, bookings can be cancelled or modified as per the cancellation policy. Charges may apply based on timing.

Is insurance included with the rental?

+

Yes, basic insurance coverage is included with every rental. Additional coverage options may be available.
